Activities

      

        

          
Learn new Technology

          
Our club offers a unique blend of learning opportunities, bringing together the worlds of Internet of Things (IoT) and Android development. Whether you're interested in developing innovative mobile applications or creating smart, connected devices, our club serves as the perfect platform for you to explore, learn, and build projects that combine hardware and software seamlessly. We focus on collaborative learning and hands-on experience, allowing members to apply their knowledge to real-world problems while working with cutting-edge technology. 

        

        

          
        

      

      

        

          
        

        

          
Android WorkShops

          
If mobile development excites you, our club provides a comprehensive introduction to Android app development. From building responsive user interfaces to working with APIs, you'll learn to create powerful and user-friendly applications. Our focus on modern tools like Jetpack Compose and Kotlin ensures that you're well-equipped to develop apps that meet current industry standards. Whether you’re just starting out or looking to enhance your skills, you'll gain hands-on experience in turning ideas into fully functional mobile applications.






          

        

      

      

        

          
IoT Workshops

          
For those passionate about IoT, our club offers the opportunity to dive into building smart devices using microcontrollers like Arduino, ESP32, and NodeMCU. You’ll explore how to connect sensors, actuators, and other hardware components to the internet, creating projects that can monitor, control, and automate processes in real-time. Whether it's home automation, smart agriculture, or environmental monitoring, you will have the tools and mentorship to develop practical IoT solutions that address everyday challenges.
